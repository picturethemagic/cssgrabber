Privacy Policy

9. How CSS Grabber processes your request

When you submit a URL, here is exactly what happens:

  • Your URL is sent to our server over HTTPS
  • Our server fetches the target page and its linked CSS and assets
  • A report is generated in memory and returned to you
  • The submitted URL and generated report are discarded immediately — we do not write them to a database or store them after your request completes

10. What we log

Our servers produce standard access logs. These contain your IP address, the timestamp of your request, and the HTTP response code. They do not contain the URL you submitted for analysis. We retain these logs for a limited period for security and operational purposes only.
